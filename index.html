Tube Me That — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What this extension does

Tube Me That is a Chrome extension that helps you launch a targeted YouTube search from any web page using a keyboard shortcut. It reads the selected text or page title to pre-fill a search query, then opens the YouTube results in a new tab.

Data collection

We collect no data whatsoever. Tube Me That does not:

  • collect, store, or transmit any personal information
  • track your browsing history or search queries
  • send any data to external servers
  • use analytics or tracking tools

Permissions used

  • activeTab — used only at the moment you invoke the extension, to read the selected text or page title. Access is not persistent.
  • scripting — used solely to read the selected text from the active tab when the popup opens.
  • storage — used to save your filter preferences (duration, style) locally on your device only. This data never leaves your browser.

Third parties

The extension opens YouTube search results in a new tab. Your interaction with YouTube is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Tube Me That has no affiliation with YouTube or Google.
